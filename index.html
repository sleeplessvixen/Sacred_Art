Discover our carefully curated collection of sacred artworks that span centuries of religious devotion and artistic mastery. Each piece tells a unique story of faith, craftsmanship, and spiritual significance.

Christ and Saint John the Evangelist

This intimate portrayal captures the profound spiritual connection between Christ and his beloved disciple. The piece exemplifies the deep personal relationship described in the Gospel of John, where the disciple is often depicted resting his head on Jesus's shoulder.

Enthroned Virgin with the Writing Christ Child

This remarkable piece depicts the Virgin Mary seated on a throne with the Christ Child engaged in the act of writing. This unique iconographic representation symbolizes divine wisdom, while the depiction of the young Jesus learning to write adds a touching human dimension to this sacred scene.

Vesperbild (Pietà)

The Vesperbild, also known as Pietà, depicts the poignant moment where the Virgin Mary holds the body of Christ after the Crucifixion. This deeply moving representation of maternal grief and divine sacrifice was a popular devotional image in medieval European art.
